About PixaMax



Brand History

Founded in 2005, PixaMax Cinemas is the largest multiplex chain spread across cities in Asia. Having its presence in 110 cities with 155 operational locations, it pledges to accord its patrons an absolute movie viewing experience. PixaMax Cinemas the brand gets its genesis from PixaMax Films Pvt Ltd, Singapore.
